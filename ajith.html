AJITH

Ajith Kumar (born May 1, 1971) is a prominent Indian actor and racing driver known for his extensive work in Tamil cinema, often referred to by his moniker "Thala" (Leader). He has starred in over 63 films since his debut in 1990, earning critical acclaim and multiple awards, including three Filmfare Awards for Best Actor – Tamil for his roles in Vaali (1999), Villain (2002), and Varalaru (2006)
